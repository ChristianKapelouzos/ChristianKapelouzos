Plov Recipe
  • History
  • Ingredients

Ingredients

  • Meat- traditional plov is made with beef,lamb, or pork
  • Rice- brown rice, jasmine, or basmati rice
  • Carrots- cut carrots into dice pieces
  • Onions- cut onions into dice pieces
  • Garlic- usea whole head of garlic
  • Water- beef broth or other stocks can be used instead of water.

Directions

  1. To begin, fry the meat. Then pour over with a broth or water and simmer until the meat is fork-tender (1 hour.)
  2. In a large pot saute the onion and carrots in oil. Add the garlic cloves, rice, and spices. fry until fragrant.
  3. Strain the meat from the simmering water into a large measuring cup. Add the meat to the rice and pour additional water into the measuring cup to make 4 cups of liquid in total. Pour into the rice and stir.
  4. Cover Plov with a lid and reduce the heat to low. Simmer for 30 minutes then turn off and steam for an additional 15 minutes.
  5. Serve!
